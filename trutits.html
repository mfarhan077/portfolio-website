Tribute to Albert Einstein

About Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein (1879–1955) was a theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics. His famous equation, E=mc², is known worldwide.

Achievements

  • Developed the theory of relativity.
  • Won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his explanation of the photoelectric effect.
  • Made significant contributions to quantum mechanics and cosmology.

Legacy

Einstein's work has had a profound impact on science and technology, shaping our understanding of the universe. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest scientists of all time.
